Hey, reader.

Welcome to my personal blog. As you read through some of the content on this page, please bear in mind that all the posts written here are primarily for my own benefit, to log my journey, record the challenges I run into, document the solutions I find. As I continue to hone my craft as a data professional, I hope you might also find something useful, regardless of the stage you currently find yourself in in your journey.

My interests are diverse, but most of the posts you will find here are related to my passion for sports analytics and data engineering. I have a particular fondness for ice hockey and the National Hockey League, but may also one day dip into a few of the other sports that continue to add rhythm to my life such as baseball, rugby or gridiron football (NFL and CFL). 

The name of the blog? This is where I currently find myself. Sat in the nosebleeds, in the bleachers, enjoying sports along with millions of other people every day. As we all continue to enjoy sports from the outside, we use data and analytics to reach, if only briefly, the joys of unpicking and deeply understand what goes on inside.

Welcome again, and I hope you enjoy your time here.
